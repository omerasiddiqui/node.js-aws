about

There is a growing community of Muslims in the area who have had to travel over 60 miles round trip to meet for prayer. Inshallah, these brothers and sisters will soon have a masjid that will offer all five daily prayers and Friday prayer, along with other teachings of The Holy Quran and The Sunnah.

By the Grace of Allah, the Town of Farmerville, Louisiana community is excited to announce plans for a masjid of our own - a place where we can worship, gather, and rejoice together.

The area is spacious enough for a men's section, women's section, wudu/bathrooms, a kitchen, and will be located on Thomas street in the heart of Farmerville.

Now we need your help to make this dream a reality!

Please help us spread the word so that we can establish our very own space by the end of Summer '19.
