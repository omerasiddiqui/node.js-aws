We are excited to announce that we are establishing the first Islamic center in Farmerville, Louisiana. There is a growing community of Muslims in the area who have had to travel 60 miles from every direction to meet for prayer. Inshallah, these brothers and sisters will now have a masjid that will offer all five daily prayers and Friday prayer, along with other teachings of The Holy Quran and The Sunnah. May Allah accept your good intentions and keep us on the straight path.
